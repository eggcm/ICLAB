Introduction

The mission of the Applied Computing Lab. (ACL) is to conduct a program of research on problems arisen in emerging computing environments, including wireless networks, mobile computing, and even newly cloud computing as well as Internet of Things (IoT), to its members. We consider the research topicss in these computing environments from the angles of data management and processing. Besides, the members may also learn or develope some new applied software technologies about Big Data processing and mobile devices. The research topics include:

  • Topics in Big Data Management
  • Data Management in Various Emerging Computing Environments
  • Issues about Ad-Hoc and Sensor Networks
  • Data Sharing in Mobile Clouds
  • Location-Based Services

  • Topics in Big Data Management
  • Data Management in Various Emerging Computing Environments
  • Issues about Ad-Hoc and Sensor Networks
  • Data Sharing in Mobile Clouds
