Join Us

Thank you for your interests in our LAB, Applied Computing LAB (ACL). We look forward to helping you to know what the objectives of our LAB are and welcome the industrious students to join us. The followings are the steps for you to join us.

  • Please browse this website and try to understand what our LAB is currently doing first. If you can not capture what our objectives are, you are welcome to send us your concerns by emails.
  • Evaluate yourself to see if your interests match our research work or the LAB objectives.
  • We emphasize the English ability. Most of the documents and papers for your study are presented in English and, in some cases, you might need to present papers in English.
  • Prospective students who want to purchase a Ph.D. Degree or have some Mathematics background are preferred.
  • The Master students are expected to have a conference paper before graduation.
  • If you want to have an interview with us, please send an email to us to setup a meeting with the email address, cmliu@ntut.edu.tw, and have your resume, transcript, project report, etc with you.
